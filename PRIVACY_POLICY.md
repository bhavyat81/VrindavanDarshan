# Privacy Policy — Vrindavan Darshan

**Last Updated:** April 8, 2026

## Overview

Vrindavan Darshan ("the App") is a devotional guide app for the sacred temples, leela sthals, and pilgrimage information of Vrindavan Dham. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** The App:

- Does **not** require user registration or sign-in
- Does **not** collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personal information
- Does **not** track your location
- Does **not** use analytics or tracking services
- Does **not** contain advertisements
- Does **not** use cookies

## Local Storage

The App stores only your **language preference** (Hindi or English) locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any server
- Can be cleared by uninstalling the App

## Third-Party Services

The App does **not** use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The App is suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
